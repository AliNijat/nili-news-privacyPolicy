Privacy Policy

Nili News
Last updated: April 9, 2026

Nili News is a multilingual news application that provides news browsing, account features, comments, likes, and localized utility features.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address when you create an account or sign in
  • Profile information you choose to provide, such as display name
  • User-generated content such as comments
  • App activity related to likes and in-app interactions
  • Approximate location, only if you grant permission, to localize weather and regional exchange-rate features
  • Device notification token, only if notifications are enabled
  • Technical information needed for authentication, security, and service reliability

How We Use Information

  • To provide app functionality such as sign-in, profiles, comments, and likes
  • To deliver localized weather and exchange-rate information
  • To send notifications if you enable them
  • To support moderation, security, and account management
  • To maintain and improve service performance and reliability

Third-Party Services

Nili News uses third-party service providers to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and backend services
  • News data providers such as GNews
  • OpenAI for content processing

These providers process data only as needed to provide the app's functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may use service providers that process data on our behalf in order to operate the app.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to enable approximate location access
  • You can choose whether to enable notifications
  • You can update profile information in the app where available
