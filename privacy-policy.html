Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nili News collects, uses, safeguards, and retains information when readers use the app, create accounts, or interact with app features and supporting services.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as email address when you register or sign in
  • Profile information you choose to provide, including display name or similar details
  • User-generated content such as comments, reactions, and related engagement data
  • App activity and service records needed to support account features and in-app interactions
  • Approximate location only when you grant permission for localized features such as weather or exchange-rate services
  • Notification tokens only when notifications are enabled on your device
  • Technical and security information required for authentication, abuse prevention, reliability, and operational diagnostics

How We Use Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the app, including sign-in, profile management, comments, and engagement features
  • To personalize localized utility modules such as weather and exchange-rate information where applicable
  • To send notifications when you have enabled them
  • To maintain platform safety, moderation workflows, account integrity, and service reliability
  • To analyze, maintain, and improve overall app performance and user experience

Service Providers and Processing

Nili News may rely on third-party service providers to operate the app and support delivery of its features, including:

  • Supabase for authentication, data storage, and backend services
  • News data providers such as GNews
  • OpenAI for selected content-processing workflows

These providers process information only to the extent reasonably required to support the services they perform for the app.

Data Sharing and Security

Nili News does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by authorized service providers acting on behalf of the app where needed to deliver features, maintain security, or support reliable operation.

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to help protect data. However, no internet transmission or storage environment can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You can decide whether to allow approximate location access
  • You can enable or disable notifications through the app or device settings
  • You can update certain profile details within the app where those controls are available

Questions About Privacy

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact the Nili News team from the App
