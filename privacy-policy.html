Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nili News handles information in connection with the Nili News mobile app and related support pages. Nili News is a news aggregation app that provides relevant local and global news from third-party sources in multiple languages, including Arabic (AR) and Farsi (FA).

What Nili News is

Nili News is an aggregator, not the original publisher of third-party articles. It displays news headlines, summaries, categories, publisher names, author information when available, source links when available, and related information to help users discover stories. Original publishers and authors retain ownership of their content.

Information we may collect

  • Account information, if you create or use an account in the app.
  • App settings, such as language preference and notification preference.
  • Device push notification tokens, if you enable notifications.
  • Comments or other content you submit inside the app.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the app.

How information is used

  • To provide app features such as language selection, comments, likes, and notifications.
  • To display news content and organize stories by category or source.
  • To respond to support, privacy, correction, and account requests.
  • To protect the app from abuse, spam, or unauthorized access.

Third-party services and sources

Nili News may use service providers for hosting, authentication, database storage, push notifications, analytics, or app operation. News content may come from public sources, publisher feeds, or news APIs. Nili News credits original publishers when displaying aggregated news.

Data sharing

Nili News does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers that help operate the app, when required by law, or when needed to protect users, publishers, the app, or the public.

Account deletion

You may request account deletion from the app or by following the instructions on the Delete Account page.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the Nili News team at support.nili@gmail.com.
