News aggregator

Nili News aggregates local and global news from third-party sources.

Nili News is not the original publisher of the articles shown in the app. It presents relevant news from third-party sources in multiple languages, including Arabic and Farsi, and credits the original publisher and author when that information is available.

What Nili News does

Nili News collects and organizes news headlines, summaries, categories, and article details from third-party sources. The app helps readers browse relevant local and global news in a clear format.

How sources are credited

Each article shown in Nili News is attributed to its original source. When available, the app displays the original publisher, author, and source link. Nili News does not claim ownership of third-party reporting.

Language support

Nili News supports multiple language experiences, including Arabic (AR) and Farsi (FA), so readers can access aggregated news in the language they choose.

Need help?

For support, corrections, publisher questions, privacy requests, or account questions, contact the Nili News team directly at support.nili@gmail.com.

Publisher transparency

Nili News is an aggregator and discovery tool, not the original publisher of third-party articles. Original publishers and authors retain ownership of their reporting, images, videos, trademarks, and other materials. Nili News displays attribution so readers can identify the original source of each article.
