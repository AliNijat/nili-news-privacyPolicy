Delete Account

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This page explains how readers can request deletion of a Nili News account, what information may be removed, and what limited records may need to remain temporarily for security, legal, or operational reasons.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send a request from the email address associated with your Nili News account
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Nili News Account
  3. Include enough account details to help the team identify the correct account safely

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account access associated with your email address
  • Profile information stored for your account
  • Associated account-level records that can reasonably be linked to your user account in our systems

What May Be Retained Temporarily

  • Limited technical, compliance, or security records when reasonably necessary for fraud prevention, abuse handling, or legal obligations
  • Backup copies retained for a limited period until deletion is completed through normal system rotation

Processing Time

Nili News aims to review and process valid deletion requests within a reasonable time after the request is verified. Some records may remain temporarily where retention is required for security, legal, or operational reasons.

Deletion Requests

Submit account deletion requests to support.nili@gmail.com.
