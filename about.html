About Nili News

Nili News is a news aggregation app that provides relevant local and global news from third-party sources. The app presents headlines, summaries, categories, source information, and article details in a mobile-friendly reading experience.

Nili News supports multiple languages, including Arabic (AR) and Farsi (FA). The app is intended for news discovery and does not replace original publishers or claim ownership of their reporting.

Publisher transparency

Nili News is an aggregator, not the original publisher of third-party articles. Articles are attributed to their original sources. When available, Nili News displays the original publisher, author, and source link.

The original publisher remains responsible for the full article, reporting, images, videos, and other source materials. If a publisher, author, or rights owner has a concern about attribution, display, correction, or removal, please contact the Nili News team.

Editorial approach

Nili News organizes third-party news for readers and may show summaries or short excerpts for discovery. Readers should visit the original publisher for the full report when a source link is available.
